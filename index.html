Henry Kids

Developed for Henry's final project.

Henry Kids is a platform for children/teens interested in programming, as well as educators who provide educational content on different technologies in the industry. All young people should have the opportunity to learn programming. They are a generation that has not had to adapt to technology because they were born with it, let's teach them to CREATE THE FUTURE. Because learning to program stimulates creativity, teamwork and problem solving, generating interest in technology. PROGRAMMING is telling a computer to carry out a task, it is explaining to your computer that you want it to do something for you..

    Doreen

    Measurement and Monitoring System.

    Measurement and Monitoring System for the prevention of pressure ulcers.

    Doreen, was the final project of the Industrial Design career where we developed a system to record the pressure distribution of patients and their characteristics. In this way, it is possible to provide information in real time and improve the quality of life of patients by helping the attending medical staff.

    Hummi

    Humidity Stabilizing System

    The project was carried out by a team of 2 members, where initially two different research topics were established per student. 1) Honey Production, 2) Temperature Regulation in Closed Spaces. After a long process of work and research, we agree on the possibility of bringing together both works with the corresponding information to propose a common product for a certain purpose (The Regulation of Humidity in Extraction Rooms).

    The work ended with an MVP and a final presentation of the model with its corresponding infographic, accompanied by an animation of the product in context.
